Time management is crucial for engineering students due to the demanding workload and complex subjects they study.

Prioritize Tasks: Use techniques like Eisenhower Matrix to categorize tasks by urgency and importance. tasks first.

Create a Schedule: Use a planner or digital calendar to allocate specific times for studying, classes, assignments, and personal activities. Stick to this schedule as much as possible.

Break Tasks Down: Divide large projects or assignments into smaller, manageable tasks. This helps prevent overwhelm and makes progress easier to track.

Set Goals: Establish clear, achievable goals for each study session or day. This keeps you motivated and gives direction to your efforts. By implementing these strategies consistently, engineering students can effectively manage their time, reduce stress, and achieve academic success.
